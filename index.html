What is HomePro?

HomePro is a home care platform that connects homeowners with professional service providers offering a wide range of home services, including repairs, maintenance, cleaning, and more.

Are the service providers on HomePro reliable and qualified?

Yes, HomePro takes great care to ensure that all service providers on the platform are reliable and qualified. Each provider goes through a background check, experience verification, and insurance check. You can also read reviews from other customers before booking a service to make an informed decision.

What if I have an issue or complaint about a service provider?

If you have an issue or complaint about a service provider, you can contact HomePro's customer support team. They will help you resolve the issue or file a complaint. You can also leave a review for the service provider so that other users can be aware of your experience.

How are payments handled on HomePro?

HomePro uses a secure payment system to process payments. You can pay for services using a credit card, debit card, or PayPal. You can view the prices for services before booking.

How do I leave a review for a service provider?

You can leave a review for a service provider after the job is complete. Reviews help other users make informed decisions when booking services. You can rate the service provider on a 5-star scale and write a comment about your experience.
